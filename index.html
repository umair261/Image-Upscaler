FAQs

❓ AI image upscaler – what does it do? - An AI image upscaler applies artificial intelligence to boost an image’s resolution while maintaining or improving fine details. The image appears clearer and more refined.

❓ What happens in AI upscaling? - The AI analyzes the existing pixels. It then predicts what the missing details should look like, using advanced algorithm. Then, it systematically fills in those details until the image’s resolution quality improves.

❓ What can I upload? - You can upload photos, digital art, scans, illustrations, screenshots of documents, and any other type of images. Our online tool support all major image file formats - JPG, PNG, BMP, AVIF, and WEBP.

❓ Does image quality suffer if I upscale? - Not with this app. Older resizing tools used to worsen image quality because they made bigger copies of the same image. The AI upscaler generates fresh pixels and details; we never make the existing ones enlarged or stretch them. So, image quality always goes up, never down.

❓ How big can I go? - Our AI image enhancer app boosts the resolution by 4 times for every upload. Need to double that figure for printing? Once upscaling is done, just upload the downloaded result. Go for further upscaling.

❓ Can I print the results of AI image upscaling? - Yes, the results are printable. The upscaled images our app provides come with higher resolutions and you can print bigger without losing sharpness.
